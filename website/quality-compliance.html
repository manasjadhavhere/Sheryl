Our Commitment

Quality & Compliance

Quality is more than a process — it is a commitment that guides every aspect of our business and every product we deliver to healthcare providers and patients.

Our Philosophy

Quality at the Heart of Everything We Do

We believe every healthcare professional and patient deserves pharmaceutical products they can trust absolutely. This belief drives us to maintain uncompromising quality standards across our entire supply chain — from raw material sourcing to final product delivery.

Our quality framework is not a compliance exercise — it is the foundation of our brand promise and the bedrock of the trust our partners and patients place in us.

End-to-end quality integration from sourcing to delivery
Partnerships with GMP and ISO-certified manufacturers only
Transparent documentation and regulatory compliance
Continuous improvement through monitoring and review

Our Framework

Quality Systems Overview

Quality Assurance (QA)

Our Quality Assurance framework ensures that every product is manufactured, documented and delivered according to defined quality standards.

  • ✓ Process validation and verification protocols
  • ✓ Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
  • ✓ Documentation control and records management
  • ✓ Vendor qualification and audit programs
  • ✓ Batch review and release procedures
  • ✓ Continuous process monitoring and CAPA

Quality Control (QC)

Quality Control verifies that products meet established specifications through rigorous testing at every stage of production.

  • ✓ Raw material identity and purity testing
  • ✓ In-process quality checks during manufacturing
  • ✓ Finished product testing against specifications
  • ✓ Stability monitoring and shelf-life validation
  • ✓ Packaging and labelling verification
  • ✓ Product integrity assessments

Regulatory Compliance

We are committed to operating in accordance with applicable pharmaceutical regulations and industry best practices across all markets.

  • ✓ WHO-GMP compliant manufacturing partners
  • ✓ CDSCO and applicable regulatory submissions
  • ✓ Comprehensive regulatory documentation
  • ✓ Transparent quality processes
  • ✓ Pharmacovigilance and adverse event reporting
  • ✓ Export compliance for international markets

Patient Safety

Patient safety remains our highest priority — embedded into every decision from product design through to post-market surveillance.

  • ✓ Product integrity throughout the supply chain
  • ✓ Safe manufacturing practices and controls
  • ✓ Reliable raw material sourcing standards
  • ✓ Proper storage and handling requirements
  • ✓ Consistent product quality across batches
  • ✓ Post-market product monitoring
Standards We Follow

Quality Standards & Certifications

Our manufacturing partners hold and operate in compliance with internationally recognized quality certifications and regulatory standards.

WHO-GMP Compliance

Manufacturing partners comply with World Health Organization Good Manufacturing Practices.

ISO-Certified Facilities

Partner facilities hold ISO certifications applicable to their manufacturing scope and capabilities.

CDSCO Approved

Products manufactured and registered in accordance with Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation guidelines.

Export Certifications

Regulatory compliance documentation for export to international pharmaceutical markets.

Pharmacopoeial Standards

Products manufactured to IP (Indian Pharmacopoeia), BP (British Pharmacopoeia) and USP standards as applicable.

Continuous Improvement

Ongoing systems, process and technology improvements to maintain and elevate quality standards.
