# Sheryl Pharmaceuticals — V2 Feature Roadmap

> This document captures all features that are **out of scope for v1** (the static website) and should be revisited once the business is ready to invest in a backend or CMS-powered platform. Reference this file when planning the v2 upgrade.

---

## 1. CMS / Dynamic Content

- **WordPress + Elementor Pro** setup (as originally referenced in the brief)
- Dynamic blog/news posts with categories, tags, and search
- Admin-editable product portfolio (add/edit/remove products without touching code)
- Dynamic careers listings managed from an admin panel
- Multi-language support (EN + regional languages for export markets)

---

## 2. Product Catalogue

- Full product detail pages with ingredients, indications, pack sizes, and images
- Search and filter by therapeutic category, form (tablet/syrup/injection), and more
- Product PDF download (brochures, technical sheets)
- Enquire about a product (per-product enquiry form)

---

## 3. Careers — Advanced

- Resume stored in cloud storage (AWS S3 / Google Drive / Dropbox API) with a secure link sent in the email — removes the file-size limitation of EmailJS
- Application tracking dashboard for HR (basic admin panel)
- Auto-acknowledgement email sent to applicants upon submission
- Job listing with dynamic posting/expiry dates managed from CMS
- LinkedIn "Easy Apply" integration

---

## 4. Contact / CRM Integration

- Zoho CRM or HubSpot integration — all form submissions auto-create a lead record
- Auto-responder emails to prospects and business enquiry submissions
- WhatsApp Business chat widget

---

## 5. Email Marketing

- Newsletter subscription with Mailchimp / Brevo integration
- Welcome email sequence for new subscribers
- Product launch email campaigns

---

## 6. Global Business / Distributor Portal

- Password-protected distributor login area
- Downloadable documents (price lists, regulatory certificates, product dossiers)
- Online business inquiry with document uploads

---

## 7. SEO & Analytics (Advanced)

- Rank Math SEO plugin (WordPress)
- Schema markup: Organization, Product, BreadcrumbList, Article
- Google Analytics 4 + Google Search Console setup
- Core Web Vitals monitoring

---

## 8. E-Commerce (Long Term)

- Institutional/B2B order portal
- Online product ordering for registered distributors
- Invoice generation

---

## 9. Interactive Global Map

- Animated, clickable world map showing export markets and distribution countries
- Country-specific landing pages for key export markets

---

## 10. Performance & Infrastructure

- CDN setup (Cloudflare or Hostinger CDN)
- Image optimization pipeline (WebP conversion, lazy loading)
- Progressive Web App (PWA) — offline access, installable on mobile

---

*Last updated: July 2026 — V1 build in progress.*
