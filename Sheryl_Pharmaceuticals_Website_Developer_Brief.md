# 6. Manufacturing Excellence

## Manufacturing Excellence

At Sheryl Pharmaceuticals, we are committed to delivering pharmaceutical products that consistently meet the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficacy. Our manufacturing model is built on strategic partnerships with carefully selected manufacturing facilities that operate in compliance with internationally recognized quality standards. By collaborating with experienced manufacturing partners, we combine advanced production capabilities with stringent quality systems to ensure every product reflects our commitment to excellence.

## Third-Party Manufacturing

Our third-party manufacturing model enables us to provide a diverse portfolio of pharmaceutical formulations while maintaining flexibility, scalability, and operational efficiency. Every manufacturing partner is evaluated through a rigorous selection process to ensure alignment with our quality expectations and regulatory requirements. This approach allows us to focus on innovation, product development, market expansion, and customer satisfaction while ensuring uninterrupted product availability.

## Partner Selection Process

Every manufacturing partner is assessed based on manufacturing capabilities, quality management systems, regulatory compliance, infrastructure and technology, production capacity, product consistency, documentation, traceability, and commitment to continuous improvement.

## Infrastructure & Technology

Our manufacturing partners utilize modern production facilities equipped with advanced pharmaceutical manufacturing technologies that support multiple dosage forms while maintaining strict hygiene, process controls, and quality standards.

## Quality Systems

Quality is integrated into every stage of manufacturing—from raw material sourcing to final product release. Our quality systems emphasize standardized manufacturing processes, batch-to-batch consistency, controlled production environments, comprehensive documentation, product traceability, and continuous process monitoring.

## Supply Chain Excellence

We work closely with trusted logistics and distribution partners to ensure timely product availability, efficient inventory management, reliable product distribution, secure transportation practices, and consistent supply to healthcare providers and business partners.

# 8. Quality & Compliance

## Quality & Compliance

Quality is more than a process—it is a commitment that guides every aspect of our business. We believe every healthcare professional and patient deserves pharmaceutical products they can trust.

## Quality Assurance (QA)

Our Quality Assurance framework ensures that every product is manufactured, documented, and delivered according to defined quality standards through process validation, SOPs, documentation control, vendor qualification, batch review, and continuous monitoring.

## Quality Control (QC)

Quality Control verifies that products meet established specifications through raw material evaluation, in-process checks, finished product testing, stability monitoring, packaging verification, and product integrity assessments.

## Regulatory Compliance

We are committed to operating in accordance with applicable pharmaceutical regulations and industry best practices through qualified manufacturing facilities, comprehensive documentation, and transparent quality processes.

## Product Safety

Patient safety remains our highest priority. We emphasize product integrity, safe manufacturing practices, reliable sourcing, proper storage and handling, and consistent product quality.

## Continuous Improvement

We continuously evaluate our systems, technologies, and processes to improve operational efficiency, product quality, and customer satisfaction.

## Certifications

Display only verified certifications applicable to your company or manufacturing partners, such as WHO-GMP compliant manufacturing partners, ISO-certified facilities, or GMP compliance, where applicable.
